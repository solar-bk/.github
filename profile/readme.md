# Solar Blackie

A hybrid Sun/muscle-powered bike!

The purpose of the __Solar Blackie__ is to:

- Use solar panels on the bike's body,
- Consume electrical energy where it is produced,
- Transport people inside cities,
- Reduce the gas emissions,
- Reduce noise inside cities,
- Motivate people to build their own bike,
- Motivate people to exercise.

# Technical specs

- 2m long, (including wheels),
- weight: about 20kg (excluding driver, including panels),
- assisted pedalling,
- motor max speed: 25km/h,
- motor (continuous) power: 250W,
- solar power: max 160W,
- battery: owner choice.

- Welding machine. I have a [Lorch Handy 160](https://lorch.eu).
